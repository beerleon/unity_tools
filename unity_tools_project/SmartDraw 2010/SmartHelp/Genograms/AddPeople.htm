Add People

SmartDraw's Automatic Shape Connectors draw lines for you and connect new symbols to others already on the Work Area, enabling you to build your visuals in record speed.

Begin by selecting a symbol on the Work Area.

Click any symbol in the symbol gallery in SmartPanel and add it as a Partner (Left or Right), Child or Parent of the selected symbol on the Work Area using the "Add" buttons.



Can’t find the symbol you want? Click on the Library Selector menu button above the symbol gallery to access more libraries (How?).

You can also add the library symbols and shapes without connecting them (How?).
