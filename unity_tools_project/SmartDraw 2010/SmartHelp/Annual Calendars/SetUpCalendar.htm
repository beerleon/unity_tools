Set Up Your Calendar

Before doing anything else, you should set the time frame for your calendar.

To specify a year for your calendar, click the Choose Year menu button in SmartPanel and select a year, or click Custom to type in a different year. SmartDraw will automatically adjust the dates to correspond to the year you choose.

You can choose whether the weeks on your calendar begin on a Sunday or Monday by clicking the Start On menu button in SmartPanel.
