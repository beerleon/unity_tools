Set Up Your Calendar

Before doing anything else, you should set the time frame for your calendar.

Monthly Calendars

In SmartPanel, click the Choose Month menu button to select the month you want. SmartDraw will automatically adjust the calendar to correspond to the month you choose.

Specify the year by clicking Choose Year and selecting a year. You can also click Custom to type in a year which is not on the menu.

Monthly calendars can also display the holidays recognized by a country of your choice. Click the Add Holidays menu button in SmartPanel and choose a country.

Finally, you can choose whether the weeks on your monthly calendar begin on a Sunday or Monday by clicking the Start On menu button in SmartPanel.

Yearly or Full Calendars

To specify a year for your calendar, click the Choose Year menu button in SmartPanel and select a year, or click Custom to type in a different year. SmartDraw will automatically adjust the dates to correspond to the year you choose.

You can choose whether the weeks on your yearly or full calendar begin on a Sunday or Monday by clicking the Start On menu button in SmartPanel.
