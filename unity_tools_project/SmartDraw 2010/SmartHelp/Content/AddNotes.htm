Add Notes

You can annotate an item on your visual to save space with text. To create a note, select the item you'd like to add the note to and click Note on the Insert tab.

A text box appears on the Work Area. Type your note into this box and click somewhere else or press the Esc (or Escape) key to close the box.

A square yellow icon appears on the item, and you can read the note by positioning your cursor over this icon.

To edit your note, click the yellow icon to reopen the text box. To remove the note, delete all of the text and close the box.

The icon is not visible when you print or export the visual - the note is only visible while open in SmartDraw.
