Close the Visual or SmartDraw

Close the Visual

To close the visual you're currently working on, but leave SmartDraw running, click the SmartDraw button (in the top-left corner of the screen), move your cursor to the bottom of the menu, and click Close.

You can also click the on the top-right corner of the Work Area (i.e., next to the Help button).

Close SmartDraw

To close the entire program, click the SmartDraw button and click Exit at the very bottom of the menu.

You can also click the in the top-right corner of the window (next to the Maximize/Restore button).
