Export to Microsoft Excel®

SmartDraw automatically creates a .SDR file when you save your visual (How?). You can also save your visual as a Microsoft Excel® file using the Quick Access Toolbar or SmartDraw Menu.

Export Using the Quick Access Toolbar

To quickly export your visual to Excel®, use the Quick Access Toolbar, located at the top left corner of the screen to the right of the SmartDraw Button.


The Quick Access Toolbar

Click the button to export your visual.

If a workbook in Microsoft Excel® is already open, your visual is placed into it. If a workbook is not open, a new workbook will open with your visual on the first sheet.

Export Using the SmartDraw Menu

You can also click on the SmartDraw Button , located in the top left corner of the screen. Position your cursor over Export, and click Excel® in the list that appears.
