Build a PowerPoint® Presentation Using Your Storyboard

Once you create your Storyboard, it’s ready to export to PowerPoint®. If you have some PowerPoint® themes, you can choose one for your presentation using the Theme menu button in the PowerPoint® group of SmartPanel.

Once you’re ready, click Build in PowerPoint® group of SmartPanel. A new presentation opens and each slide in the Storyboard becomes a slide in the presentation, with the elements appearing as bulleted text. If you insert any graphics, they also appear nicely laid out on the slide.
