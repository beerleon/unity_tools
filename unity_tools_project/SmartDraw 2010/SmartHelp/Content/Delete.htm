Delete an Item on the Work Area

You can delete an item on the Work Area by selecting it and pressing the Delete key.

Most SmartPanels may also have a specialized "Delete" or "Remove" button for your particular visual. In these cases, you can click on an item and click the respective button in SmartPanel.

If your visual uses Automatic Shape Connectors (i.e., when you add a symbol, it is connected by a line to another symbol), deleting a symbol will also delete all symbols which are subordinate to it. The other branches will automatically adjust to fill the space left by the deleted symbol(s).
