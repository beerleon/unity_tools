Convert Your Visual to a Project Chart, Mind Map, or Timeline

You can quickly switch between Project Charts, Mind Maps, and Timelines so you don’t have to rebuild your data after creating one of these visuals.

To do this, click the View As menu button in SmartPanel and choose another visual.

Layer tabs will appear below the Work Area so you can quickly switch between visuals.

Any subtask in a Project Chart becomes a subtopic in the Mind Map, and vice versa. The Timeline will simply show main tasks, topics and milestones, but not subtasks or dependencies.
