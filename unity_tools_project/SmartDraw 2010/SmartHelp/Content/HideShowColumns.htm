Hide or Show Some Units of Time

Hide a Column

There may be some units of time which you want to temporarily hide. To do this, select at least one cell in those columns in your visual and click Hide Column in SmartPanel.





The gridline where a hidden column had been will be slightly thicker.

Show a Hidden Column

To see the columns again, select at least one cell in each of the columns on either side of where the hidden column was and click Show Column in SmartPanel.






Note: If you are making a Project Chart, you can also hide the table section columns (such as "Resources") by clicking Properties in SmartPanel (How?).
