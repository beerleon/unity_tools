Add or Change Arrowheads

You can add and customize arrowheads on any line in your visual. Select the line or lines (How?) you want to change.

Then click Arrowheads in the Tools group of the Home tab and choose from the menu.


If you have a vertical line, choosing Right from the menu that appears will put the arrowhead on the bottom of the line, and vice versa when you choose Left.

Click Custom to change the appearance, position, or size of the arrowheads.

If you're changing the arrows on Automatic Shape Connectors, the entire chain or branch will have the same arrowheads, since they are really "one line".
