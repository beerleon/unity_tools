Add or Delete Rows and Columns

If you add a table (How?) to your visual, you can use the buttons in the Rows & Columns group of the Table tab to add rows and columns.

Begin by clicking a cell of the table. Then click Insert Left and Insert Right to add a column respectively to the left or right of the selected cell's column.

Clicking Insert Above and Insert Below will add a row above or below the row containing the selected cell.

To delete a row or column, click a cell of that row or column and click Delete in the Rows & Columns group of the Table tab. Then choose Remove Row or Remove Column from the menu. This menu also gives you the option to delete just the contents of the row or column.
