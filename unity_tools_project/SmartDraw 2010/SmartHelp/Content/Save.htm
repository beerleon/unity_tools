Save a Visual

To quickly save your visual, click the Save button on the Quick Access Toolbar, located to the right of the SmartDraw button.


The Quick Access Toolbar

This will open the familiar "Save" dialog box prompting you to name your visual and choose a location to save it to on your computer. Once you click Save, the visual is saved as a .SDR file.

You can also click on the SmartDraw Button in the top-left corner of the screen and then click Save, which will open the same "Save" dialog box.


You can also save your visual to file formats other than .SDR (How?).
