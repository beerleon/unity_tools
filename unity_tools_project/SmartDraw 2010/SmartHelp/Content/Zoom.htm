Zoom Your View of the Visual

You can use the Zoom tool to view your visual at different ranges. You might want a closer view to read text or move items with precision. But while coloring or organizing your items, you might want to see the entire visual at once.

To change the zoom level of your visual, click on the percentage button next to the magnifying glass button at the top left corner of the Work Area.

In the menu that appears, you can choose from one of the percentages, or click on Custom to specify your own zoom level.

You can manually zoom into your visual by clicking on the magnifying glass button. This changes your cursor to a magnifying glass. When you left-click on the Work Area, you zoom in by 25%, centered on the point you click. To zoom out, right-click instead.

To leave zoom mode, just press the Esc (or Escape) key on your keyboard.
