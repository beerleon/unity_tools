Email Your Visual as a SDR or PDF

To email your completed visual as a SmartDraw (SDR) file or PDF, directly from SmartDraw, click the SmartDraw Button and position your cursor over Email.

A menu appears in which you can choose to send your visual as a SDR (SmartDraw) or a PDF file by clicking on the respective option.

Your default mail program will automatically open a new email message with your file attached.

Note: If you just click Email, a PDF version of your visual will attach to a new email automatically.
