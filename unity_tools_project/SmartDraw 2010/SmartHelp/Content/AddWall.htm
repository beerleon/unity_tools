Add a Wall

To add your first wall, click Add Walls in SmartPanel. Then click on the Work Area where you'd like to begin drawing the wall. Drag your mouse and release once the wall is the desired length.

The dimension of the wall is shown as you draw, making it easy to tell when to stop.

To add a second wall, begin drawing at the end of the existing wall. SmartDraw will automatically connect the two walls. You can continue in this manner until you’ve drawn what you want.

To stop drawing walls, click Stop Adding Walls, where "Add Walls" had been.

You can also press the Esc (or Escape) key to stop adding walls.

You can rotate the entire wall frame, including anything inside the walls, using its rotation handle (How?).
