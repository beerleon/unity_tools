Find More Symbols

SmartDraw offers an extensive selection of symbol libraries with hundreds of symbols to add to your visual.

To search the libraries, click the Library Selector menu button in the Build group of SmartPanel, just above the symbol gallery.

You can choose a new library in this menu or search for other libraries by clicking More... to open the "More Symbols" dialog box.

Click on a library to preview it. When you've found the library you want, click it and click OK. The library will appear in the symbol gallery, ready for you to add its shapes (How?).
