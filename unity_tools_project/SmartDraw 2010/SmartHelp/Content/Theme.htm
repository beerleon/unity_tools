Choose a Theme

The first thing to consider when you color your visual is the theme. A theme color-coordinates your visual so it appears professional and pleasing right away.

The theme overrides any manual coloring you apply, so you should apply a theme before making any color changes.

To apply a theme, click the lowest arrow button in the Theme group of the Home tab and select a theme from the gallery.








Your visual is instantly changed!

The only coloring technique that is not completely overridden by changing themes is the Quick Style.
