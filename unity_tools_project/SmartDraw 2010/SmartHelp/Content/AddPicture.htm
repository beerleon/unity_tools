Add a Picture

You can add a picture:

  • Anywhere on the Work Area
  • Inside a shape or table

Add a Picture Anywhere on the Work Area

Before adding a picture to the Work Area, make sure no items are selected.

To add the picture, click Add Picture in SmartPanel and use the dialog box that appears to browse your files for the picture you want to insert.

Select the picture and click Open to insert it. Click on the picture and drag it to position it as you like.

Add a Picture Inside a Shape or Table

To add a picture inside a shape or table, click on the shape or table cell to select it.

Then, click Add Picture in SmartPanel, search for the picture in your files, and click Open.


Replace a Picture

You can replace a picture by selecting it and inserting a new picture.

Edit the Picture

To edit the inserted picture, use the Picture tab (How?).
