Move Walls or Symbols in the Work Area

Moving walls is not like moving other items in the work area. You can:

  • Move the Wall Frame
  • Move a Wall
  • Move Other Items in the Work Area

Move the Wall Frame


To move the intact frame of walls, click Move Outline in SmartPanel, click the frame and drag it to a new position.

Move a Wall

When a wall is part of a wall frame, you can click and drag the wall to move it. When you do this, the other walls stretch to stay attached.



Move Other Items on the Work Area

Every other item can be moved by clicking and dragging it to a new position.
