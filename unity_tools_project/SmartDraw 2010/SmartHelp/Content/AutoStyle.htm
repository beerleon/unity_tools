Turn AutoStyles On or Off

SmartDraw's AutoStyle feature colors your visual in a way that helps the viewer to read it.

When AutoStyle is active, shapes on each hierarchy level have their own color and style.

If you prefer a color scheme for your symbols that doesn't change color with levels, uncheck the Use AutoStyle checkbox in SmartPanel before you begin adding symbols.

You can change the colors using Themes, Quick Styles, and more (How?).
