Change Shape and Line Colors and Styles


Change the Fill Color of a Symbol

To change the fill of a symbol, click on it, or several symbols (How?), then click Fill in the Shape Style group of the Home tab and choose from the menu.

Not all library symbols can be filled properly due to the nature of the symbol, but we recommend you try changing the fill and line color of a symbol to see how it handles these actions. You can always undo! (How?)

Change the Color and Style of a Line or the Border of a Symbol

To change the color of the border of a symbol or the color of a line, select the symbol or line and then click Line in the Shape Style group of the Home tab (next to the Fill button). This menu also contains menus for choosing the thickness and dashing pattern of the selected line(s).

Note: Changing the theme will override any coloring and styling using the above methods.
