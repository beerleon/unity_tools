Export to PNG

Saving your visual in the usual way (How?) automatically creates a .SDR file, but SmartDraw allows you to save your visual as several other popular file types, such as PNG, PDF, JPEG, HTML, GIF, Microsoft Word®, Excel®, PowerPoint®, and several more.

To save your visual as a PNG, click on the SmartDraw Button , located in the top left corner of the screen, position your cursor over Export, and click PNG in the list that appears.

In the dialog box that appears, find the location to which you want to save your visual and give it a name. Click Save to finish.
