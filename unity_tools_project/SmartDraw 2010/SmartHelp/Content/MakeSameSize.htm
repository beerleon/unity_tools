Make Symbols Uniform in Size

You can make your visual appear more professional by making symbols the same height, width, or both. Begin by selecting your symbol(s). To do this, hold the Shift key while clicking each symbol you wish to change.

The last symbol you click will have black handles and be used as a reference point for the other symbols to resize to. When you make the symbols the same size, all other symbols will resize to the last symbol you click.

To apply the resizing to your symbols, click Make Same and choose from the menu in the Shape Layout group of the Design tab.
