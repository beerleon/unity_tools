Add a Line to Connect Two Symbols

You can anchor lines to symbols so the line remains attached to the symbol when you move it.

Begin by clicking Draw Lines in SmartPanel. This transforms your cursor into a line-drawing tool, with the "Shape Connector" line shape selected.

Next, click on the edge of the shape from which the line will originate.

Click on the symbol you wish to connect to. As you approach the symbol, Connection Points appear along its border, indicating points where you can attach the line. Your cursor becomes an anchor icon, indicating that the line will remain attached to the symbol, even if you move the symbol.

To add text to the line you've just added, begin typing.


You can also just draw a line by clicking and dragging in the Work Area.
