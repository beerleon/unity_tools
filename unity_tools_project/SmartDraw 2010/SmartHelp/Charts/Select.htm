Select Parts of the Chart

You can select an item by clicking on it; once an item is selected, black or white square handles appear along its border or line.

Select a Series

Click on any bar, point, or slice of a series to select the entire series.

You can also click on the swatch of the legend (if it is visible) to select a series.


Select a Category

To select a category, click just below the axis of the chart under the category.


Select an Axis

Click anywhere on the line of an axis to select it.
