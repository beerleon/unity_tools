Reorganize Series

To change the order of series within a category, simply click on a series and drag it to a new position within the category.




Pull Pie Charts and Relative Value Charts Apart

You can drag a slice of a Pie Chart or Relative Value Chart away from the others for a more separated look.

To do this, simply click on a slice and drag it away from the others.
