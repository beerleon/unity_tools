Add a Graphic to a Slide

You can add a graphic, such as another SmartDraw visual, to each slide of your visual. When you build a Microsoft PowerPoint® presentation with your storyboard (How?), the graphic will be placed on the corresponding slide.

Begin by clicking on a slide to select it.

Click Add Graphic in SmartPanel and use the dialog box that appears to browse through your computer's files for the graphic you want to insert.

Select the graphic and click Open.


You can replace a graphic already in a slide simply by clicking on that slide and adding a new graphic. The old graphic is automatically removed.


Remove a Graphic

To remove a graphic from a slide, click on the slide and click Remove Graphic in SmartPanel.
