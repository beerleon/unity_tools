Add or Delete a Step

SmartDraw's Automatic Shape Connectors draw lines for you and connect new symbols to others already on the Work Area, enabling you to build your visuals in record speed.

Add a Step

Click on the step to the left or above where you'd like to add a new step.

Then click Add Step in SmartPanel.

The step is automatically added and the other steps adjust to accomodate the new step.


Delete a Step

To delete a step, simply click it and click Remove Step in SmartPanel. The other steps adjust to fill the deleted step's place.
