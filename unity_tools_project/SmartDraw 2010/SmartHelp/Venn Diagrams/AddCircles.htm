Add More Circles


To add another circle, begin by clicking on a circle symbol in the gallery in SmartPanel.

Then click in the Work Area to add the circle.

Click and drag a black square handle to resize the circle appropriately.

Then, drag the circle into position. If you are creating a Venn Diagram, you may need to reposition the existing circles.

If you are creating a Target or Onion Diagram, hold the Shift key and click on each circle in the visual. Then, in the Design tab, click Align and choose Centers. Click the button again and choose Middles. This will cause your circles to be concentric.
